The processes of transitions between gas, liquid, and solid states are called:

1. Deposition: The process where a gas turns directly into a solid.

2. Sublimation: The process where a solid turns directly into a gas.

3. Condensation: The process where a gas turns into a liquid.

4. Evaporation: The process where a liquid turns into a gas.

5. Freezing: The process where a liquid turns into a solid.

6. Melting: The process where a solid turns into a liquid.

These processes occur due to changes in temperature and pressure conditions
