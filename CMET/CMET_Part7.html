Wind Directions:

True Wind: Reported in METAR/TAF/GAF/GPWT.（True:所有看到的，除了打印出来的AWIS/ATIS）

Magnetic Wind: in AWIS/ATIS/ATC.（Magnetic: 所有听到的+跑道编号）

AWIS/ATIS:

AWIS (Automatic Weather Information Service): Typically used at non-towered or remote airports;

ATIS (Automatic Terminal Information Service): Typically used at towered or busy airports.

Some definitions：

Surface Air Temperature:

Thermometer placed 1.25–2 m AGL, shaded, and vented.

Ground vs. Air:

Sunny: Ground temperature > Air temperature.

Cold: Ground temperature < Air temperature.

 

Prevailing Visibility (VIS): >50% of the observed area meets the reported VIS.

 

Rainfall:

RF00.0/000.0 mm:

First value: Rainfall in last 10 minutes.

Second value: Rainfall since 9 AM local time.

 

Runway Visual Range (RVR):

Measured by ground instruments for IFR operations.

VFR Requirement: VIS >5 km.

INTER/TEMPO & FM/BECMG

FM/BECMG

FM (From): Rapid change (e.g., FM 151230Z, after 12th hour, weather will change). DDHHMM

BECMG (Becoming): Gradual change (e.g., BECMG 1523/1601, weather will change between 23th and 01st hour of next day, but will surely change after 01st hour of next day). DDHH

INTER vs. TEMPO

Term

Duration

Definition

INTER

<30 minutes

Weather occurs intermittently (multiple short periods, separated by breaks).

TEMPO

30–60 minutes

Weather occurs temporarily (one or more periods, total ≤50% of the timeframe).

Common Mistake Clarified

Example: INTER 1600/1612 MOD RAIN

Misinterpretation: "Only one rain event lasting <30 minutes in the 12-hour period."

Correct Interpretation:

Multiple short rain events (<30 mins each) can occur between 1600–1612.

Rain starts and stops, with breaks in between (e.g., 10-min rain at 1600, 15-min rain at 1630).

Operational Requirements (OPRs)

VFR Day: Flights >50 NM; VFR Night: All

Fuel Requirements: Sufficient for alternate/holding until conditions clear.

Conditions Triggering OPRs (within 30 minutes of flight):

Cloud >½ below 1,500 ft or TS present.

VIS <8 km.

Crosswind > aircraft limits.

 

Exam Question: Alternate or Holding? :

Holding: Short-term delays (e.g. <1 hour).

Alternate: Required for longer (e.g. >3-hour) disruptions.

Note: 考试有单选/多选题，有些题可同时 Holding 和 Alternate

Note：考试时，建议采用两种不同的方法以作为相互验证。 “全计数法”，即只要直线穿过某个格子，无论面积大小，都将其计入总数。 “面积法”，即只有当直线穿过格子的面积超过该格子面积的1/4时，才将其计入总数。

Advisories & Communication

SIGMENT: Severe/widespread (e.g., TS, turbulence).

AIRMENT: Moderate but significant (below 10,000 ft).

ATIS: VHF broadcast (similar to METAR, magnetic wind).

VOLMET: HF broadcast on request (METAR/TAF for selected airports).

Hazard Alert: ATC-managed (requires two-way communication).
